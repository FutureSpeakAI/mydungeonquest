---
name: MyDungeon redaction law
description: Journal redaction events are the authority; snapshot flags are advisory; derived artifacts fall whole when any cited turn is struck.
---

**The rule:** redaction truth lives in the journal — `redaction` events strike turn ordinals via turn-record k ↔ `logs[k]` alignment. The `log.redacted` snapshot flag is only an advisory belt. Every consumer (Storybook, Podcast Forge, future exporters) must re-derive strikes from the journal and apply BOTH courts.

**Derived artifacts fall whole:** a Chronicler page (or any retelling that cites a turn range) may only be used while EVERY cited turn is still bound; one struck turn strikes the whole passage, and the consumer falls back to lawful raw sealed content. A page that cannot prove its range (no citations) is never used. Individual sealed quotes remain lawful if their own turn is bound, even when their page fell.

**Why:** redaction can land AFTER a retelling was sealed; checking only quotes/turns let struck content ride back in through the passage prose (architect-caught in the podcast, matching the storybook's earlier fix).

**How to apply:** any new feature that replays, prints, voices, or exports sealed content re-derives struck ordinals from the journal and gates both raw turns and derived passages before rendering.

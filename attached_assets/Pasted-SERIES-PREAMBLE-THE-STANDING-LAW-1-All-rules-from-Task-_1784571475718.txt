SERIES PREAMBLE: THE STANDING LAW

1. All rules from Task 52 through the most recently closed task carry in full: log every test edit in LOOP_LOG.md under the current task's heading, never weaken an assertion to pass, never mock a judge, never add skip or fixme, no new dependencies beyond those already sanctioned, and missing keys are a hard stop.
2. DIRECTIVE FIRST. Every build task begins by writing its EXPERIENCE-DIRECTIVE file at the game root, stating each design decision as law with its enforcement point and its gate, before any code is written. If the build must deviate, the directive is amended in the same commit with a one-line reason.
3. THE PROTOCOL IS ADDITIVE. New story operations may be added; existing operations, reducers, and validator behavior may not change except where a task explicitly sanctions it. Older campaigns must load and play untouched.
4. THE MOCK TIER IS SOVEREIGN. Every new feature ships a deterministic mock path so the entire keyless chain runs with zero keys. The pinned number may only grow, each task states its exact growth, and the G13 assertion moves in the same commit as the gates that grow it.
5. THE SINGLE DOOR STANDS. However many minds deliberate, exactly one validated dm_turn enters the record per turn.
6. THE EXTENSION LAW stands as pinned: once per task, only on a ledger with zero undiagnosed reds and zero open game defects, only to bank already-validated cures, frozen, one strike. THE MINT LAW stands as pinned: in the proving harvest only, a required plate whose ladder ends refused or anchored may re-lay its full ladder with fresh dice, up to three complete ladders per artifact per iteration, every attempt attested, fidelity proven or the door refuses; all-three-fall is blocker-grade and stops the hunt; no warden floor, court bar, or paint-law byte moves under it. LISTLESS LEGISLATION stands: prompt-law strings contain no example enumerations, and the poisonSweep keeps its relics byte-stable.
7. THE INSTRUMENT LAWS stand: forced-binary verdicts with the scalar as diagnostic only; every new judge question passes the calibration probe to perfect separation on known goods and bads before its court sits, and its final text is pinned byte-stable; the two-stage magnified look governs any small-feature examination; the cache-freshness law names REPLAY in every run log; probe before cure on any infrastructure red; a court may stand down only under the recusal-under-custody law, with the bench's calibration table present on the standing protocol proving the named separation, a recusal without custody being a skip; and THE CONTROL LAW stands: every constructed control verifies its own lie before it seats, is re-forged from the paint-and-probe budget when it cannot, and a tooth whose control cannot be made to lie is invalid until it can.
8. THE RITUAL CLOSES EVERY TASK. Keyless green at the new pin, then the full proving loop (harvest with all three terminals first-class, all courts in parallel, zero skips, every standing tooth per the teeth ledger biting where its inputs changed) green three consecutive times, then the architect review on the full diff with findings fixed or refuted on house law, then the closing commit. When any stop condition's letter and its stated purpose diverge, the hunt halts and the house rules; the agent never self-interprets.
9. THE REPORT is one message in the standing format: iterations against the ceiling, the three greens with exit codes and zero-skip statements, the criterion table with EXECUTED, teeth, the yellow ledger, THE RECUSAL LEDGER naming every recusal with its custody proof, the LOOP_LOG section, the keyless tail at the pin, the CHANGELOG entry verbatim, and the closing commit hash. End with the closing sentence only if it is true.

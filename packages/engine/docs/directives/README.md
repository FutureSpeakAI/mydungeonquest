# The directives shelf

The eight directives here — VII through XIV — are the table's later cuts,
ratified before any code under the series' directive-first law, and ported
whole with the law they govern. Their law fractions live in this engine's
seats; the table (MyDungeon.Quest) keeps its React, database, and provider
halves, judged at its own gates.

Two lineages share the earlier numbers, and honestly:

- **The workspace shelf** (`docs/directives/` at the monorepo root) holds
  the engine's founding line: EXPERIENCE-DIRECTIVE (I) through IV, V "The
  Saga Cut", VI "The World Cut", and the REMAKE-DIRECTIVE. The engine's
  saga and world groundwork answer to that V and VI.
- **The table's series** (the game repo's root) runs IV through XIV. Its
  IV is byte-identical to the shelf's IV; at V and VI the lines diverge —
  the table's V is "The Hooked World" (0.6.0) and its VI is "The
  Possessions Cut" (0.7.0), whose law also lives here (the trove and
  purse seats).

Nothing was renumbered: a directive's number is part of its ratified text.
Read the numbers with their lineage, and both lines stay true.
